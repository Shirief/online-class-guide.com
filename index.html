What Is Online Class?

Online Class

What is it?

An online classroom is an environment created through use of a learning management system that allows students and teacher to connect either synchronously (real-time, with teacher and students meeting at the same time or asynchronously with interaction between teacher and students occurring intermittently with a time delay; teacher students are generally separated by location.

Purpose

The Importance

Online Class enables the teacher and the student to set their own learning pace, and there's the added flexibility of setting a schedule that fits everyone's agenda. As a result, using an online educational platform allows for a better balance of work and studies, so there's no need to give anything up.

It make more easier to choose a program of your choice because you can choose them through online unlike on an actual situation choosing a program that you like is hard because some universities are not available through the course or program that you like.

4. Convenience

In Online Class the education or the learning system are way more convenient for people who lives on rural areas, people have disabilities and people who had trouble in financial expense Online class help their life a bit easier.

In Online Class there’s a few ways where you can join and learn in any classes, there are your typical Classroom setting like an actual classroom where there are many participants or the student and one adviser or instructor and the other one is a one-on-one where there’s only one student and one instructor.

9. Unique learning environment

In Online Class have a Unique learning environment that can help the student to be at ease, get rid of their fear to be question on the spot, Online Class give student more time to response, when there are comfortable to give feedback, and the content or the lesson wont progress when you’re not ready.

10. New and exciting technology

Disadvantages

1. Online student feedback is limited

In Online Class/Online Learning system the Instructor or teacher feedback towards the student are limited because most student are dissatisfied with the lack of personal feedback, it may be of the internet or any social media medium not all instructor are willing to give their personal email address that’s why student have to struggle having a feedback, while on a face-to-face scenario the instructor give immediate feedback to the student during on a class hours or dedicated office hours.

2. Online Class/E-Learning can cause social Isolation

The Online Class/E-Learning methods may often cause or leads to several mental health issues because of lack of interaction towards other people, because you are learning by yourself and you just facing your screen on many hours, while on face-to-face we are practicing our self for a better interaction towards other to bust our self-esteem and we lest feel anxiety, negative thoughts and heightened stress on interacting with others.

3. Online Class/E-Learning requires strong self-motivation and time management skills

Online Class/E-Learning can be had lack of self-motivation because of the environment of were there live, there are many nuisances in us on place some may not have of does while on the face-to-face scenario there are many things that can keep you motivated like face-to-face communication with other students or communication with your instructor and you have strict schedules that we keep on the track during studies or learning hour.

4. Lack of communicational skill development in online students

Online Class/E-Learning System are lacking of having a face-to-face communication toward other people like your classmate or you Instructor/professor because there are some roles that you need to mute yourself because of our own surroundings have a lot nuisances that’s way we can’t enhance our communication skill, while on a face-to-face scenario we can communicate with help of interaction towards other people.

5. Cheating prevention during online assessments is complicated

Online Class are easy to do some cheating scenario because you are only facing your screen you are alone at a time that no one can caught you while on the face-to-face scenario our Instructor/Professor are guarding us to prevent on doing cheating.

6. Online instructors tend to focus on theory rather than practice

The Online Class system are focusing on theoretical lesson because it’s the only thing available due to lack of interaction with other people that’s the reason why some online class skip any practical activity, while on a face-to-face scenario we can learn more than theoretical lesson because of having an interaction.

7. Online Classes Are Prone to Technical Issues

Some Online Class disadvantages are technical issues some causes of Online class disruption are bad internet connection, a bad audio, bad video interface and some are power outage by these issues student and the Instructor/Professor are the one to need to adjust on these situations, on the other hand face-to-face scenario the only things that are needed are yourself.

8. Online Class/E-Learning is limited to certain disciplines

The Online class/E-Learning system tends to be more suitable with other program like humanities and social science because most of the lesson are theoretically while other programs like medical science, engineering, criminology, are required for a heavy hands-on activity for more experience there no amounts of online class or lecture can substitute an real-life industrial training or an autopsy for medical students and some demo crime scene for criminology student, that’s why all educational discipline are not equal to online class.

9. Online Class/Online learning is inaccessible to the computer illiterate population

Not all student has their own personal device like smart phone or personal computer like desktop or laptop because some may face financial problem and some may have device of there own but have problem because of old device and bad internet connection specially on rural areas.

10. Lack of accreditation & quality assurance in online education

Some people say the Online class are more effective than a traditional classes because of fast learning system, an easy time management but there are some online class are unaccredited because all the materials are being checked by instructor/professor themselves as a such poor quality assurance and lack of accredited of online providers such as university.
